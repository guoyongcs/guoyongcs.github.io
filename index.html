About me

I am an assistant chief scientist at Huawei and lead a research team. Previously, I worked as a post-doctoral researcher at Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), working with Prof. Bernt Schiele. Before that, I obtained my PhD degree from South China University of Technology. My research interests include machine learning and computer vision. I also served as the reviewer for several top-tier conferences and journals, such as NeurIPS, CVPR, TPAMI, etc.

Internship & Collaboration

I am looking for self-motivated intern students that have an interest in my research areas. There is always room for new ideas and projects and I am happy to discuss them with you. When you are highly motivated, please send your CV to my email guoyongcs@gmail.com.

What you can get as an intern:

  • In-depth guidance towards publishing papers in top-tier conferences/journals
  • Sufficient computational resources
  • Competitive internship salary

It is also acceptable to build up a remote collaboration but you cannot get the intern salary in this case.

[Note!] The intern application is only acceptable between March and September in each year. BTW, you can always reach out for remote collaboration at any time.
