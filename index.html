About me

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Computer Vision and Machine Learning Group in Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF). I obtained my PhD degree with the School from South China University of Technology. My research interests include machine learning and computer vision. I served as the reviewer for several top-tier conferences and journals, such as NeurIPS, CVPR, ICML, TPAMI, TIP, TNNLS.
