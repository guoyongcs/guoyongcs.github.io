About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate with the School of Software Engineering at South China University of Technology. I also received my bachelor degree in Software Engineering from the same university in 2016. My research interests include machine learning and computer vision.

I served as the conference/journal reviewer for TIP, NeurIPS, AAAI, UAI, MICCAI.
