About me

I am an assistant chief scientist at Huawei and lead a research team. Previously, I worked as a post-doctoral researcher at Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), working with Prof. Bernt Schiele. Before that, I obtained my PhD degree from South China University of Technology. My research interests include machine learning and computer vision.
